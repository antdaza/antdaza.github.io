Registering a Full Node

Earn rewards and help secure the ANTD network by registering your own full node. This process is similar to Oxen's service node setup.

Requirements

  • A synced antd-daemon running with --full-node
  • Enough ANTD to meet the staking requirement
  • Your operator wallet address (from antd-wallet-cli)

Steps

  1. Start your daemon with: 
    ./antd-daemon --full-node
  2. Open a screen session: 
    screen -S fullnode
  3. Inside the daemon console, run: 
    prepare_registration
    Follow the interactive prompts:
    • Choose individual staking: y
    • Enter your wallet address
    • Enable auto re-staking if you wish
  4. Copy the full output command (e.g.): 
    register_full_node 1234abcd ...
  5. Open your wallet: 
    ./antd-wallet-cli
  6. Paste and run the command: 
    register_full_node ...

Auto Registration (Optional)

If you want your wallet to auto-renew your node registration every 30 days:

register_full_node auto ...

Note: Auto registration will push the wallet to the background. Use screen -ls to manage processes.

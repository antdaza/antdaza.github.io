About ANTD Coin

ANTD Coin is a privacy-first cryptocurrency built on the Monero codebase. It goes beyond financial transactions by enabling secure, decentralized communication directly on the blockchain.

Messages & Articles

ANTD Coin lets users post messages and articles directly on-chain. Messages are limited to about 100 words to ensure efficient storage and privacy.

Use the CLI command add_article to publish a message or article. You can also pay a publisher to post your content by sending them the required amount.

Future versions will support longer posts and enhanced metadata.

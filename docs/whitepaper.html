ANTD Coin: The Whitepaper

ANTD Coin is a privacy-first cryptocurrency based on the Monero codebase, with advanced on-chain messaging, decentralized publishing, and reward-driven infrastructure. This whitepaper outlines the core principles, architecture, and future direction of the ANTD ecosystem.

Introduction

In a world increasingly surveilled and censored, ANTD Coin emerges as a platform where financial transactions and ideas can flow securely and anonymously. Building upon the proven technology of Monero, ANTD adds a unique feature: the ability to post messages and articles directly on-chain.

Privacy & Core Technology

  • RingCT – Ensures hidden transaction amounts.
  • Stealth Addresses – Protects recipient identities.
  • Decentralized Messaging – Users can embed articles/messages (~100 words) inside `tx_extra` securely.
  • Optional Publishing – Users can pay known publishers to post on their behalf.

Message & Article Layer

Using the CLI `add_article` command, anyone can publish a message on-chain. These articles are immutable, timestamped, and fully decentralized. Each post contains a title, content, publisher tag, and cryptographic content hash.

This feature makes ANTD not just a currency, but also a censorship-resistant publishing network.

Developer APIs

Articles are accessible through RPC using the get_show_article method, allowing wallets, explorers, and third-party apps to display article metadata like title, publisher, and content hash.

Node Participation & Rewards

ANTD encourages community participation by rewarding users who run full nodes. These nodes serve the network, validate transactions, and may eventually host message data for lightweight clients.

Future Vision

  • Support for longer articles using segmented `tx_extra` formats.
  • Integration of encrypted content and access policies.
  • Tokenized incentives for publishers and node operators.
  • Decentralized moderation tools and content categories.
